VISUAL APPEARANCE INSTRUCTIONS ONLY

This website must look heavy, dominant, and industrial.
It must feel like a large, established roofing company.
It must NOT look like a startup.
It must NOT look smooth or playful.
It must NOT look like a marketing agency.
It must NOT look rounded and soft.

Overall Visual Identity:
- Hard edges.
- Strong alignment.
- Clean grid.
- Bold typography.
- Large spacing.
- Clear section separation.
- No decorative gradients.
- No glass effects.
- No soft UI elements.
- No trendy UI effects.
